Typhoon HIL Simulator Device Introduction

Typhoon HIL simulator devices are dedicated hardware devices that enable you to perform real-time testing when paired with our Typhoon HIL Control Center (THCC) software. HIL simulator devices have a wide variety of built-in ports and interfaces that allow you to connect to your real control hardware under test at low voltages. Additionally, these devices can have their functionality extended through HIL Interfaces, such as the HIL Connect interface, and HIL Accessories depending on your testing needs.
