Power Systems Toolbox

Schematic Editor functionalities available through the Power Systems Toolbox

Real-time only: This document is valid only for real-time/VHIL simulation.

The Power Systems Toolbox is an additional toolbox that supports real-time modeling of key power system components to ensure high fidelity of your system-level models. Its main purpose is to enable you to build your microgrid or electrical subsystem models in a way that mimics the final deployment to maintain high-fidelity in testing. These include real-time functionality for models of Exciters, Governors, Turbines, and Stabilizers which would typically be deployed to maintain high power quality.
