## [HIL dSpace Interface Cards](https://www.typhoon-hil.com/products/hil-interfaces/dspace-interface-cards/)

To help kick-start your control development with dSpace rapid control prototyping (RCP) devices, Typhoon HIL offers a selection of HIL dS interface cards. Using a HIL dS Interface Card provides seamless plug and play experience where all necessary analog and digital inputs and outputs are routed to Typhoon HIL4 and HIL6 series device front IO connectors.
