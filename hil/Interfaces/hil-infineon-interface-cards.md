## [HIL Infineon Interface Cards](https://www.typhoon-hil.com/products/hil-interfaces/hil-infineon-interface-cards/) 

Typhoon’s HIL Infineon Technologies TriBoard Interface Card was specifically designed to enable a seamless Plug and Play interface between AURIX™ TC3xx and TC4xx evaluation boards from Infineon and any of the HIL4 and HIL6 series real-time simulation devices. The TriBoard Interface Card directly plugs into a single row of DIN41612 connectors, found on any of our HIL devices.
