## [HIL CCS Interface Card](https://www.typhoon-hil.com/products/hil-interfaces/hil-ccs-interface-card/) {/* #hil-ccs-interface-card */}

The HIL CCS Interface card is a dedicated module for testing the smart charge communication of electric vehicles and electric vehicle supply equipment. The module can simulate both communication partners, the electric vehicle supply equipment (EVSE) and the electric vehicle (EV) itself
